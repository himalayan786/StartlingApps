Course Offered:
UIIT imparts instructions for Becholar of Information Technology (B.Tech(I.T))

Entarnce Examination (Fresh Entry (1st semester)):
Those candidate who fulfill the elgibility criteria (given below) will be called for entrance examination. Entrance Examination consist of two papers, each of two hours duration. In each paper, candidate will be required to answer 100 questions of Multiple Choice Type detailed below.

  • PAPER-I
    • Physics (50 MCQs)
    • Mathematics (50 MCQs)
  • PAPER-II
    • Aptitude with Logical Reasoning (40 MCQs)
    • IT related GK (40 MCQs)
    • English (20 MCQs)

Admission Criteria for Fresh Entry Candidate (1st Semester):

  • Eligibilty: 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics and any one of the other elective subjects (with 50% marks for GEN and 45% for SC/ST) from any Board/University established by law.

Admission Criteria for Lateral Entry Candidate (3rd Semester):

  • 3 years diploma (after 10+3 or metric) from Himachal Pradesh Board of Technical Education or equivalent with minimum 60% marks in aggregate in IT/CSE.
  • A candidate must have passed B.Sc, with Mathematics and Physics as subjects with minimum of 60% marks in aggregate from HPU, Shimla or any other University/Institutions located in Himachal Pradesh.
  • A candidate seeking admission under sponsored catagory under the lateral entry scheme should have smae eligibility as given above in (a) or (b).

Number of seats:

  • Fresh Entry: 60 + 6* + 1 (single girl child)
  • 6* (in i) indicates for 10% of sactioned intake approved by AICTE, to provide consession/programme fee waiver for women economically backward, physically challanged meritorious students in the ratio of 2:3:1 as per AICTE norms.
  • Lateral Entry: 6 + any seats remaining vacant at 2nd year level.
