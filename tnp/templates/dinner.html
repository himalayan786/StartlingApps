Annual Dinner
'Goodbyes, they often come in shades, sometimes in emotions, sometimes in flavours'

Annual Dinner is an occasion of ecstatic jubilation which brings together the entire UIIT community under one roof to commemorate the diverse activities ranging from the achievements of students in the academic arena including the co-curriculuar and the extra-curricular realms to bidding an adieu to the senior year students. UIIT celebrated its 12th Annual Dinner on 30th June, 2012, against the backdrop of presto music and burnished lighting.
